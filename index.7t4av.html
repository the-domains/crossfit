A Certified CrossFit Trainer:
Designs and
leads CrossFit
workouts for
both groups
and
individuals, from beginner
to
advanced, that are safe,
effective,
and
appropriate
for
individuals
who are
apparently healthy or
have
medical
clearance to
exercise. The trainer
recognizes
the
limitations of his or her
own
knowledge and skill set
and
refers
clients
to other healthcare
professionals
when
appropriate.
