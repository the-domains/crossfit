The elevated push-up is a

Start with the feet on a

increase height of feet.
The athlete must maintain

through the entire range of
motion. If this is too
difficult choose a
standard
push-up or go to the
knees.

as possible in 10 minutes
of: 20 box
jumps, 24-inch
box/20-inch box* 5
handstand push-ups 5
push-ups Post
rounds
completed to comments.
For the
intermediate who
has some handstand
push-
ups the repetitions per
round are
decreased to

without
decreasing the

If

2

8 elevated
